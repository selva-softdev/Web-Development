Display Flex property - "Justify-content":

  • The justify-content property aligns the flexible container's items on the main-axis (horizontally).

  • Default value: "flex-start".

  1. Justify-content:"Flex-start"

    Items are positioned at the beginning of the container.

    Example:

    Item 1
    Item 2
    Item 3

  2. Justify-content:"Flex-end"

    Items are positioned at the end of the container.

    Example:

    Item 1
    Item 2
    Item 3

  3. Justify-content:"Center"

    Items are positioned at the center of the container.

    Example:

    Item 1
    Item 2
    Item 3

  4. Justify-content:"Space-around"

    Items will have space before, between, and after them.

    Example:

    Item 1
    Item 2
    Item 3

  5. Justify-content:"Space-between"

    Items will have space between them.

    Example:

    Item 1
    Item 2
    Item 3

  6. Justify-content:"Space-evenly"

    Items will have equal space around them.

    Example:

    Item 1
    Item 2
    Item 3

  7. Justify-self:

    • The justify-self property specifies the alignment for the selected item horizontally(x-axis) inside a grid container.

    • The justify-self property aligns a grid item within its grid cell in the inline direction.

    • Justify-self values:

      • Left
      • Right
      • Start
      • End
      • Flex-start
      • Flex-end
      • Stretch
      • Center
